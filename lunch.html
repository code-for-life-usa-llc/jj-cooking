Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza got its name from the use of pepperoni
as the main topping over pizza. Pepperoni used
to be one of the major topping for popular dishes in many Italian-American
restaurants. When pizza became a popular dish in these restaurants,
they started to serve pepperoni as a topping on their pizzas. This
practice slowly took momentum marked the beginning of pepperoni pizza.
