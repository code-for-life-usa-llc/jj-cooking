Recipe

  • 1¼ lbs. russet potatoes
  • ¼ tsp. Kosher salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • ⅓ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 strips cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp. chopped green onion

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray.
  2. When finished, remove from oven and pour potatoes into a greased casserole dish. Add cheese, crumbled bacon and Hidden Valley® Jalapeño Bacon Ranch. Mix well and bake for another 10 minutes, until cheese is melted. Remove from oven and top with chopped green onion.
